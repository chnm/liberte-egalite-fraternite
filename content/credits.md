<p>The following credits are from the original site, launched in 1998 and retired in 2018. Amanda Morton (with support from the Louise and Rudolf Fishel Fellowship) and Sean Takats migrated <em>Liberty, Equality, Fraternity</em>'s content to a more modern platform and replaced most of the images with higher resolution versions from the public domain.</p>
